Return Policies

30-Day Return Policy

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If for any reason you are not satisfied, you may return the item within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. Please ensure that the item is unused, in its original packaging, and in the same condition as you received it.

Damaged or Defective Items

In the unfortunate event that you receive a damaged or defective item, please contact our customer service team within 7 days of receiving the product. We will arrange for a replacement or refund as per your preference. Please provide detailed information and, if possible, photos of the damage to expedite the process.

Non-Returnable Items

Certain items are non-returnable for hygiene or safety reasons, or due to their nature. These include but are not limited to:

  • Personalized or customized products
  • Perishable goods such as food or flowers
  • Health and personal care items
  • Downloadable software or digital products
  • Gift cards or vouchers

Please review the product description carefully before making your purchase, as non-returnable items will be clearly indicated.

Return Process

To initiate a return, please follow these steps:

  1. Contact our customer service team to obtain a return authorization and instructions.
  2. Package the item securely, preferably in its original packaging.
  3. Include all accessories, manuals, and documentation that were originally included with the product.
  4. Ship the item back to us using a trackable shipping method.
  5. Once we receive the returned item, our team will inspect it and process your refund or exchange accordingly.

Please note that return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer, unless the return is due to our error or a defective item.
